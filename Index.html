Our Mission

Our mission is to use baking to instill a sense of self worth and an awareness of ability to contribute to society in individuals with developmental disabilities.

Our Purpose

Mental health needs in Vermont and in the United States as a whole, are not being sufficiently met. Too often people are labeled and written off, and it is our goal to instead embrace and support individual with developmental disabilities. We hope to demonstrate that our participants are capable of being contributing members of society despite their differences.

Why Baking?

We decided to use baking because it contains therapeutic value as well as providing our participants with a marketable and practical skill.
