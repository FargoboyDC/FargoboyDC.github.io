First Session of Baking for Joy Completed in Brattleboro

We have successfuly concluded our first set of classes at the Brattleboro Co-op. We had classes ranging from four to seven particpants whose baking profiency and independence grew rapidly in a few short weeks. A few students were even able to move ahead of the Baking For Joy instructors at times. Some of the baked goods we have prepared in class are gluten free strawberry shortcake, chocolate peanut butter cookies, and whoopie pies.

Help Us Grow

We want to partner with more organizations who serve our participants and help more communities. If your communnity could use our programs and you would like to help us get started contact us. We are also looking for more volunteers for our existing site.

Why Baking?

We use baking because of it's proven therapeutic value as well as providing our participants with a marketable and practical skill.
